Cake Stand:

A cake stand is a piece of tableware, typically a plate on a pedestal, used for displaying and serving cakes, pastries, or other treats. It can also be a kitchen tool used for holding cakes during decoration. Cake stands come in various materials and designs, including multiple tiered versions.
